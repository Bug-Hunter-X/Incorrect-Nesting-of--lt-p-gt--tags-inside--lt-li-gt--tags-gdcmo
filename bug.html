This is a common HTML error, but often overlooked. It involves incorrect nesting of elements, specifically, placing a <p> (paragraph) tag inside a <li> (list item) tag incorrectly. While it may seem to work visually, it's semantically wrong and can cause issues with accessibility and styling.

Example of incorrect nesting:

 <ul>     <li>This is a list item. <p>This is a paragraph within the list item.</p></li> </ul>
